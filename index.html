Wayanad – Complete Travel Guide

Wayanad is a scenic hill district in Kerala known for misty mountains, waterfalls, wildlife, trekking trails and ancient caves. Perfect for honeymoon, family holidays and adventure trips.

Edakkal Caves

Ancient caves with prehistoric carvings dating back to 6000 BC. Offers a short trekking experience and panoramic views.

Banasura Sagar Dam

India’s largest earthen dam surrounded by lush hills. Boating and speed boat rides available.

Soochipara Waterfalls

Three-tier waterfall ideal for trekking and swimming.

Pookode Lake

Beautiful freshwater lake surrounded by forest. Pedal boating available.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Home to elephants, deer, leopards and rare bird species.

Chembra Peak

Highest peak in Wayanad. Famous heart-shaped lake trek.

Kuruvadweep Island

Protected river island with bamboo rafting and nature trails.

Thirunelli Temple

Ancient temple located in Brahmagiri hills, spiritual destination.

Meenmutty Waterfalls

One of Kerala’s largest waterfalls with adventurous trekking route.
